ENHYPEN

Learn more about the upcoming leaders of the 4th generation era of K-POP: ENHYPEN

About

Originating in South Korea, ENHYPEN is a 7 member K-POP boy group formed by the entertainment company Belift Lab (a subsidary of HYBE Corporation) from the survival show I-LAND. The group debuted on November 30th, 2020 and consists of members Jungwon (leader), Heeseung, Jay, Jake, Sunghoon, Sunoo, and Ni-ki.

Known for their captivating performances, intricate choreography, and diverse musical range, ENHYPEN quickly captured the hearts of many fans (ENGENES) all over the world and has become a leading force in the 4th generation of K-POP. Their music explores themes of youth, self-discovery, and growth, deeply resonating with their global audience. As the they continue to break barriers and expand their reach, ENHYPEN is solifying their place as one of K-POP's rising stars.

Discography

ENHYPEN's discograpy showcases their versatility and growth as artists, blending genres like hip-hop, EDM, R&B, rock, and pop. Since their debut, the group has continuously delivered hit songs and chart topping albums.
